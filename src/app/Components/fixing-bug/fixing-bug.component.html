omg

Starts the BugFixer CLI — your go-to tool for identifying and resolving bugs in your project.

fixer

Once inside the BugFixer CLI, select the fixer option to begin the bug-fixing process.

Enter prompt

After selecting fixer, you'll be prompted to describe the issue you're facing — specify where and what the problem is — then hit Enter. The tool will automatically analyze, fix the bug, and rewrite your code.
